Shelter

Directories:

Homeless Shelter Directory (HSD)
  • Directory of all homeless shelters and services for the needy.
Homeless Resources for Oakland, CA
  • List of shelter, food, health, work, legal, library, spiritual, exercise, laundry, clothing, recovery, and senior citizen services.

Organizations:

Crossroads Emergency Housing with EOCP
  • A green multi-service emergency housing facility in Alameda County.
Operation Dignity
  • Assists homeless veterans and their families by providing emergency, transitional, and permanent housing for the homeless in Alameda County.
CityTeam Ministries
  • Provides hot meals, safe shelter, showers, clean clothing, medical services, and recovery programs to the homeless population.
DreamCatcher Emergency Youth Shelter and Support Center
  • Provides shelter and support services for homeless, runaway, and at-risk youth ages 13-18.
